About Our Space

Ham Lab is a community-driven makerspace where technology enthusiasts, amateur radio operators, and makers come together to create, learn, and share. As an official endof10.org member, we're committed to open-source innovation and collaborative making. Our doors are open to everyone who wants to tinker, build, or just hang out with like-minded individuals.

What We Offer

3D Printing Powerhouse

State-of-the-art 3D printing farm with multiple printers for rapid prototyping and production

Quadcopter Workshops

Build and fly your own drones with hands-on workshops and flight testing

PlayStation Gaming Corner

Kid-friendly gaming zone with PlayStation for recreation and learning

Electronics Lab

Professional soldering stations and testing equipment

Starlink Internet

High-speed satellite internet connectivity for all your projects

Ham Radio Station

Complete amateur radio setup for enthusiasts

Phreaking Corner

Telephone and network hardware exploration area

Linux Installation

We install and prefer Fedora Linux! Part of our endof10.org initiative to promote open-source computing

Coffee Corner

Because good hacking requires good coffee
